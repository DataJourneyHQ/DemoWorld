## Task: Movie Review — "Project Hail Mary" (Andy Weir)

Write a **detailed, engaging movie review** of *Project Hail Mary* by Andy Weir, written as if the book has just been adapted into a major Hollywood film.

Your review must include:

1. **Opening hook** — grab the reader in 2-3 sentences
2. **Plot summary** (spoiler-free) — set the scene without giving away the ending
3. **Character analysis** — focus on Ryland Grace and Rocky, their chemistry and how their relationship drives the story
4. **Science accuracy** — how well does the film handle the real astrophysics, biology and chemistry concepts (astrophage, the Tau Ceti system, relativistic travel)?
5. **Emotional beats** — what moments land, what moments feel rushed
6. **Comparison** — briefly compare to other sci-fi adaptations (e.g. The Martian, Interstellar, Arrival)
7. **Verdict** — a punchy final rating out of 10 with a one-line closer

**Tone:** Smart, witty, enthusiastic — like a reviewer who genuinely loves hard sci-fi but also wants a good cry.
